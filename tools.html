Primary Tool

KVFX Algorithm

The KVFX Algorithm is a proprietary indicator suite designed to identify directional bias, structural confirmations, and expansion opportunities across multiple timeframes.

Rather than attempting to predict price, the algorithm identifies when repeatable structural conditions align — providing a systematic basis for entry decisions within identified zones.

TradingView Multi-Timeframe Pro + Elite Access
  • Directional bias indicator — identifies the dominant structural trend on current and higher timeframes
  • Structural confirmation signals — validates that price is reacting at a significant level
  • Momentum filter — distinguishes genuine expansion from low-probability moves
  • Zone proximity alerts — notifies when price approaches a mapped WhisperZone
  • Entry validation layer — multi-condition confirmation before generating a signal output
  • Integrated macro filter — suppresses signals during unfavorable macro conditions
Framework Tool

WhisperZonez Indicator

The WhisperZonez indicator maps structural zones directly onto your charts — visualizing supply areas, demand areas, accumulation bands, and liquidity pools identified by the framework methodology.

Designed to eliminate subjective zone drawing and provide a consistent, rules-based visualization of market structure across all instruments and timeframes.

TradingView All Timeframes All Tiers
  • Automated zone identification — demand and supply zones mapped based on structural criteria
  • Multi-timeframe zone display — zones from D1 and W1 overlaid on lower timeframe charts
  • Liquidity pool markers — visualizes areas of concentrated stop orders
  • Accumulation band detection — highlights quiet ranging areas before expansion phases
  • Zone strength rating — differentiates high-confluence zones from weak ones
  • Customizable visual settings — adapt display to any charting style or preference
